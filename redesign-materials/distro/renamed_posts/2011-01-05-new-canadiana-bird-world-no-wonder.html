Bird World
No Wonder
(Self Released)
Halifax, NS
::web/sounds::
From the wondrous forms of Aaron Levin: A dreamy, psychedelic gauze wraps Bird World's debut into a crunching bundle of 90s excursions. The soaring vocal hooks and burning guitars flutter around strange pop-psych flirtations, cultivating a bewildering anti-genre of catchiness. Sinusoids of this calibre originate from only one source: Halifax. Bonus points for being Long, Long, Long-related. Spectacular cover art, so grip with the quickness.
