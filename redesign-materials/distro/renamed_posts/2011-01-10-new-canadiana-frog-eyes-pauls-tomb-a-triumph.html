Frog Eyes
Paul's Tomb: A Triumph
(Dead Oceans)
Victoria, BC
::web/sounds::
From the living thunder of Joni Sadler: Frog Eyes are veterans at the art of crafting challenging and weird pop music and Paul's Tomb reaches the pinnacle of their craft, capturing all of their trademark elements: Carey Mercer's warbling howl, Melanie Campbell's staccato drumming, and the winding, roller-coaster narratives, boiling all of them down to their most potent state. They don't waste a second with the daring, nine-minute opener; there is no fat to be trimmed anywhere on this record. Mercer and co. may well be polar opposites to the myriad of nonchalant chill-fi pop bands floating about the blogosphere, and god bless 'em for it - they're playing music full of emotion and heart, and never in their history have they sounded better doing it.
