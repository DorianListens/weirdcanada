Ruddy Guy
Best of Microcassette Archives
(Self Released)
Vancouver, BC
::web/sounds::
From teenage surgery of Aaron Levin: Blown-, hallowed-, adjective'd-out basement moves from this Vancouver-via-Calgary mystery. Immediately addictive juvenile pop frequencies with an imagination rarely seen in the genre emanate from the compilation of 4-track demos recorded between 2008-2009. The dreamy, fragile soundescapes will drift through your mind, leaving traces of Ruddy Guy's pop agility in every neural transmitters. I'm not really sure how you can grip this, but you should try.
