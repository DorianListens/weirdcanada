Metz
Negative Space
(We Are Busy Bodies) ::web/sounds::
From the megative zpace of James Goddard: Negative Space's blistering, all-offense Punk'n'Grunge proudly pushes Metz's noise crusade forward. Opening with a series of carefully timed guitar shrieks - like a call to the ramparts of the pre-medieval French city with which they share their name - and continuing with relentless drumming, six-stringed wizardry, Constantinian vocals, and what might be the auditory equivalent of burning oil, the cuts on this gorgeously coloured vinyl are taking no names as they storm your aural countryside. This is the last of their three 7" opening salvo, get in on the ground floor.
