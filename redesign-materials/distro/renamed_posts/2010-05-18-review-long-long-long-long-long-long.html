Long, Long, Long
Long, Long, Long
(Self Released)
Halifax, NS
::web/sounds::
From the loins, loins, loins of Aaron Levin: From the ashes of York Redoubt's blitzkrieg through Canada's art-pop continuum, Long, Long, Long have emerged a deliciously sauteed phoenix. More ambitious, weird, angular, and, dare I say, funkier (maybe?) than York Redoubt, Long, Long, Long is a wild continuation of the brilliant stream of pop cognizance festering in Canada's mathematical east. Long, Long, Long is a marvel of price-per-hook insanity and is brimming with narrative, noise, and every pop sensation known to humankind. I suggest you grip. NOW. A+++(infinity).
