Brazilian Money
Doing What I Want EP
(Totally Disconnected)
Edmonton, AB
::web/sounds::
From the macaroni make-out party of Jesse Locke: For their inaugural platter, Edmonton’s newest 7” slingers dish up this piping hot four song EP from the boys in Brazilian Money. Former Mitts/Wicked Awesomes! bassist Garrett Johnson is the focal point of these friendly neighbours’ seam-splitting double-drumming skronk squall, driven by hooky holler-along choruses, sassy cackling multi-tracked vocals and even a chug-a-lug acoustic shanty in closer “Bones.” 300 copies, and worth its weight in reais.
