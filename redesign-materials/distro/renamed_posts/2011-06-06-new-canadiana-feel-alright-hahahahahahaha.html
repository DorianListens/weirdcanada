From the serene pink of Aaron Levin:
With toes (and microphones) dipped all over North America, Montreal-by-way-of-Calgary's Feel Alright despoil the bountiful splendor of granulated catch. Craig Fahner's vocal vacation sails dreamily, steady groovin' along the sun's riff-o-rays as our subverted ear plunges into the infinite garden of a hook-filled mind. Top of the pops! Lovely pink J-Card. A grippers delight!
