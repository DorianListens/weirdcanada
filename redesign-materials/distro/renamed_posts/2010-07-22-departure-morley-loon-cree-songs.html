Morley Loon
Cree Songs
(CBC Northern Service Broadcast Recording)
??, NWT
Originally Released: 197?
From the northernly service of Aaron Levin: Mystic folk-psych drifting between a Native American tradition and the first wave of acoustic lysergia. Morley's intimate Cree singing is augmented by woven strands of ethereal flute, hand percussion, and melancholic guitar, conjuring a dense empathetic miasma. One of the most affecting records I've ever heard. Found in a pile of CBC Northern Broadcast Recording 7"s I received from a friend and the only of its kind I've ever encountered. If you know anything about Morley Loon (who unfortunately passed away some years ago), please get in touch. A short history of recording sessions in this series can be found here (PDF). 500 copies pressed and distributed as promos.
