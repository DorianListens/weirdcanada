Cold Warps
Cold Warps
(Self Released)
Halifax, NS
::web/sounds::
From the pure-pop-for-now-people hook-factory of Paul Lawton: Brilliant AM radio power-pop that is spot on in so many ways: the songs are simple, the production timeless, the guitar solos sparse, and the back-ups economical. Absolutely nothing is spared; everything cleaned and sacrificed at the alter of the almighty hook. I don’t want to hear power-pop that doesn’t have hooks, and this tape has enough hooks to deprive you from sleep. There is a charming innocence here, and I’m excited to hear where they take the canon they've learned here.
