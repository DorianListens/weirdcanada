Construction & Destruction
Mutatis Mutandis
(The Quarantine)
Port Greville, NS ::web/sounds::
From the mutant mutandis of James Goddard: Rural art-rockers radiating traces from a secluded homestead hanging over the Atlantic Ocean. Fresh like the crunch of first steps in burnt snow. Mutatis Mutandis is Construction & Destruction's fourth bass-led caravan of hyper-text lyrics, dense guitar riffs, detached drumming and Kohakian meditations. It takes a moment to reach the place they're coming from. Venture forth and see for yourself.
