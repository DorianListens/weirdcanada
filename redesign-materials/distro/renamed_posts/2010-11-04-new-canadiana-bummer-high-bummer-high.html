Bummer High
Bummer High
(Southpaw Records)
Vancouver, BC
::web/sounds::
From the wintering heights of Aaron Levin: Wispy, dusted country of an ilk only brewed north of the border. Way north. Snowy cowboys crossing the infinite tundra, riding their horses into the cold oblivion. With sharp stabs of frozen twang, reverberating beneath harrowing vocals recorded in the bowels of an ice cave, Bummer High's debut cassette gives you a glimpse into the weathered lives beneath coats of frozen leather.
