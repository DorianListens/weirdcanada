U.S. Girls // Dirty Beaches
Split 7"
(Sibling Sex)
Philadelphia, USA // Montreal, QC
::web/sounds:: // ::web/sounds::
From the american shunnery of Aaron Levin: Dirty Beaches' low-bit explorations through reverb hell have reached an all-time high on the synesthesia scale. Bubbling, smokey pulses of burnt low-end colour his spectrum of hazy crooning and rolling bass-lines like never before. The proto-apocalyptic rock and roll has become a psychotic ride through slow, droning waves of mystery and fear. Songs like "16 Coaches Long Akai Tape" will permeate your grey matter and desecrate any low-light associations, mercilessly running through your mind onwards. The split 7" is beautifully paired with Philadelphia's U.S. Girls.
