Wealth Plant
Friends
(Yew Nork)
Calgary, AB
::web/sounds::
From the wealthy foliage of Jesse Locke: Young turk Jean-Sebastien Audet has been making waves as of late with his reverb-rock duo Fake Fur, hip-hop beat project Zouk Fuck and micro-label Yew Nork, yielding this skewed solo offshoot. Lab Coast affiliate Shawn Dicey lends his production skills to the Friends cassette for a tape-hiss-laced teenage riot brimming with ragged guitar riffs, ghostly vocals and various indefinable sound samples. It’s all full steam ahead until mellow Ducktails-esque closer “Bible Fruit” coasts to the shore.
