Travis Bretzer
Saucy Tasters
(Self Released)
Edmonton, AB
::web/sounds::
From the dry blazers of Aaron Levin: Hiding amongst Edmonton's not-so-secret posse of twin spring wielding pop crooners, Travis Bretzer explodes with a liquid soft-rock catch that will drag you through the primordial pop goo, resonating with every natural frequency as you shake with anticipation for every track. It's the completely unpunk, hook-driven AOR gold that hits the secret parental force burrowed in all of us. Strange attractions indeed. MUSTGRIP.
