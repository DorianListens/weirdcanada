Secret Pyramid
Ghosts
(Self Released)
Vancouver, BC
::web/sounds::
From the hidden rhombi of Aaron Levin: Minimalist journeys through static debris and processed guitar form the blueprint to this Solars side project hailing from Vancouver. The dense new age grooves on Ghosts are a fitting sonar trigger to your mental mars vacation; calming reverberations through cosmic fog, dripping vocals and mystic drones. Listen with three ears... or three hands.
