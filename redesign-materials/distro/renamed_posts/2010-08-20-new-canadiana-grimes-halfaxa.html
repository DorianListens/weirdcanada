Grimes
Halfaxa
(Arbutus Records // Self Released)
Montreal, QC
::web/sounds::
From the cosmic slop of Aaron Levin: If Grimes' tour CDR (to be officially released via Arbutus on September 30th at Pop Montreal) is any indication, the cosmic-pop deity is transcending into a vocal-cruising eidolon of celestial proportions. Layers of wyspy vocals form the cooling galaxies of Halfaxa while her minimal drums synthesize Enja's lingering espers into a club anthem fit for the Restaurant at the End of the Universe. Or the Mos Eisley Cantina. Either way, Grimes' future-stream sinusoids are peeling wigs.
