Slim Twig
A Sheik in Scores
(Self Released)
Toronto, ON
::web/sounds::
From the skewed pop of Aaron Levin: Beneath the reverberated echoes of Slim T's hazy swagger undulate the shards of pop cultures theatrical past. Culled from hours of vintage soundtrack LPs, A Sheik in Scores is the third volume in Slim Twig's musically bedded explorations. Sway to the wildly dense psychedelic metronome as punchy beats carry the skewed pop sensibilities into a timeless oblivion of baroque swashes, broken guitar leads, and busted grooves. Packaged beautifully in a micro clam-shell. Grip to the max.
