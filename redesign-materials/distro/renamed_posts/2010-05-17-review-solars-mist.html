Solars
Mist
(Prairie Fire)
Vancouver, BC
::web/sounds::
From the misty fire of Aaron Levin: Winnipeg's Prairie Fire boldly capture Solars' dronic embers on this fantastic voyage within the sublime realms of electronic nullism. Delicate, aquatic guitars hover above the pulsing washes of Mist's b-side, while "solder + sons" drills deep within our cosmic electrolytes. Stay for the full ride and bring your mind to pure psychic parity. Both tracks were recored live.
