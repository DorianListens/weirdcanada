Alex Durlak
Rural Route No. 4
(Standard Form)
Toronto, ON
::web/sounds::
From the rural nodes of Aaron Levin: Alex Durlak's submission to Standard Form's 3" MiniCD Rural Route series is a heavy, heavy, heavy transmigration into the shearing heights of processed guitar. Alex takes every fibernetic mode to a synthetic extreme, processing this single-take improvisation through a jungle of granular synth patches to produce the dense, meditative excursion. Recorded at the tail end of a bleak Canadian winter, this is as deep as it gets. Wonderfully packaged in the eco-friendly, non-glue fold-out. Heavy grippage.
