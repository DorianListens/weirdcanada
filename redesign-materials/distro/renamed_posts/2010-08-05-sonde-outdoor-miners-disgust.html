Outdoor Miners
Disgust
(Pop Echo Records)
Edmonton, AB
::web/sounds::
From the disgusted mind of Aaron Levin: The latest diamond-tip offering from Edmonton's autarchic trio is a convergence of the varied streams binding their sound. Their wilding drummer grips the mic for "Bent Brain," a searing foray into classic punk riffery (complete with anthemic, pit-grinding chorus), while the scalding mellowness of bass-face Sagar pierces through "Friends With Money." The whole spectrum is collected in "Disgust," which just happens to contain an over-the-top classique rock solo. Another brilliant travelogue into the adjective-rock cosmos. ?GRIP(to infinity).
