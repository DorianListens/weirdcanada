The Pop Winds
Understory
(Self-Released)
Montreal, QC
::web/sounds::
Wind is an apt word for this paisley psychedelic pop trio originating in Ontario (now residing in Montreal). Canada's industrial heartland is a strange place for music of this kind and much like Jarvis Street Revue, Thunder Bay's out-of-freaking-nowhere garage-psych freak-fest, The Pop Winds defy Ontario's cold, grey mien to deliver sweeping currents of breezy drum-machine pop. It's the perfect fall record; a mellow travelogue contrasting the darkness of Canadian winters on the horizon.
