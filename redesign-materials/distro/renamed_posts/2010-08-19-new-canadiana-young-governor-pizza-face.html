Young Governor
Pizza Face
(Scotch Tapes)
Toronto, ON
::web/sounds::
From the ate too many chocolate bars of Aaron Levin: Young Governor soars to anthemic heights on this six minute post-pizza buffet. Its jangly rhythm bursts with gusto, jamming the radio glam treachery of cult heros into a reverb-soaked elucidation. This day-time special is crafted for all your teenage fantasies; let it seep into your pores and shake the foundation of your pubescent ethos. A fantastic voyage into 80s pop-punk anthemy. Hand-cut lathe on "pizza glass" by Scotch Tapes. Sounds rad at both speeds. Eat it up!
