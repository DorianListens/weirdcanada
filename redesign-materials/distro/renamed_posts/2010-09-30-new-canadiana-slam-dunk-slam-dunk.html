Slam Dunk
Slam Dunk
(Fan Club Music Club)
Victoria, BC
::web/sounds::
From the bear cub of Joni Sadler: Slam Dunk may still be fairly young as far as bands go, but this gang of scrappy Victorians has certainly lost no time in recording some killer jams and taking the road by storm. With gang-shouted choruses, twisting fuzz guitar melodies, and seemingly endless energy, these dudes are proving seriously hard not to love. Viva Slam Dunk, indeed.
