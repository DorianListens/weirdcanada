Highest Tide

From solo shreddery of Aaron Levin: Divorce Records CEO takes off to the coast and blows out a wild set of solo Jew's Harp shreddery, leaving us with eight-teen minutes of reverberated oralic intensity. Siamese sinusoids of deep twang and chaotic warble form a meditative sheen of mystic harmony that will entrance and enlighten. Very few copies left, so grip while the night mirror lasts!
