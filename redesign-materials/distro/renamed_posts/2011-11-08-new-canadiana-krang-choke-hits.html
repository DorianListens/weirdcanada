From the dad blues of Jesse Locke:
Billowing clouds of Bob Hope waft from the airbrushed van of these riff-razing moccasin rockers and their illicit Iommion smoke ring. Choke Hitsâ€™ marathon blazers outpace even Electric Wizard yet maintain enough movement throughout to avoid basement couch burnout. Amidst the ashen embers of gnarled guitar damage, hi-hats crackle and tom rolls pop, as far off (and out) electronics hover like UFOs in the hazy heavens over Roswell. Call it The Chronic 2011.
