The Peelies
Together Forever
(Lava Dance Records)
Montreal, QC
::web/sounds::
From the jingle-jango of Aaron Levin: Post-Yé-yé garage jangle is rarely exported outside of Quebec, an odd fact given the adorable narrative Bande à Part threw together on Montreal's The Peelies. Their debut album, Together Forever, has a summery, dissonant clangor and (oddly) prairie chic perfect for drawing all over your bud's face and lighting a car on fire. Together we'll douce the flames with a squinted, morning warble; it's an indian summer.
