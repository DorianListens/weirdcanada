The Friendly Dimension
Choose Your Own Adventure
(Self-Released)
Halifax, NS
::web/sounds::
From the unidimensional freak show of Aaron Levin: Take a collection of B-sides and rarities by a group with only one official release and you have a snapshot of our wasted youth. Choose Your Own Adventure is a voyeurs guide to summers spent trolling sidewalks for personal anthems. There is truth in the dirty concrete beat to death by the endless footsteps of teenagers. Visit any mall and you'll hear it: resilient visions of psychotropic folk-rock, catalysts for our lazy, veiled teendom, and subconscious grooves pulling us back to summers of hidden cigarettes and hairspray secrets. We've all been teenagers bored to death by a world with too much to offer. Let The Friendly Dimension be your guide. If you want to enter their audible vortex, click here.
