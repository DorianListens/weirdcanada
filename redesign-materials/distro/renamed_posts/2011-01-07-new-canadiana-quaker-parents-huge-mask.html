Quaker Parents
Huge Mask
(Self Released)
Halifax, NS
::web/sounds::
From the wondrous forms of Aaron Levin: Micro bursts of power pop jammery. Dusted skirts of minor-key mellow. Punching, drawing, blinding, swiping, soothing, Huge Mask fires pop quanta at a bewildering, Haligonian gallop. It's a Parisian gnu-wave of genre cut-ups and catchy brilliance. Another nugget of Long, Long, Long relation.
