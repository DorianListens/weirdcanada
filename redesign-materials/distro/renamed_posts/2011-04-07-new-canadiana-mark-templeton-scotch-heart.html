From the guru static of Aaron Levin:
Drags of electrostatic enigma fluctuate on the periphery of Scotch Heart. Mark Templeton's latest offering is a dense mass of ambient exhortation. Throughout the short c20, melodies wither into quad-screwed chorals laced with an endless, undulating current of drones, static, and processed lullabies. The sweat lodge appropriately recommends listening in total immersion. Blissfully gripped!
