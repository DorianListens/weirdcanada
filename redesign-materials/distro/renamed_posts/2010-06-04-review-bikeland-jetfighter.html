Bikeland
Jetfighter
(Self Released)
Calgary, AB
::web/sounds::
From the Albertan twangs of Aaron Levin: Blown-out shit-punk with some strange Albertan twang that is violently addictive. What it lacks in fidelity is made up with sheer attitude as the vocalist tears through the tape's magnetic digitalia, shrieking within Alberta's desolate underground. A dense layer of red-line pop imagination surrounds Jetfighter, while it's sonic liquidation wrenches ear drums and blows speakers.
