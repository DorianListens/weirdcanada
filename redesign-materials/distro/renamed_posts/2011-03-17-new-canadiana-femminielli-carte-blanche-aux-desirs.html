From the softcore erotica of Jesse Locke:
Under the blinding stage lights, Carte blanche aux désirs illuminates Bernardino Femminielli’s complete transformation from levitating shaman to the sexiest man in subterranean show biz. Whether whispering, commanding or crooning in his signature lusty leer, Femminielli’s vocals follow in the Gainsbourg tradition of aged lechery set against an alien chorus and skin-chilling space-scape of pulsating synths. It’s the soundtrack to a future softcore erotica permeating the now thanks to Montreal’s newest tape label, Los Discos Enfantasmes.
