GOBBLE GOBBLE
Wrinklecarver b/w Nemo
(National Archive of Records)
Edmonton, AB
::web/sounds::
From the neo-fabric of Aaron Levin: Shards of electrostatic brilliance pepper the cover of GOBBLE GOBBLE's prodigious digi-pop wonder. Their latest 7", which has permanently littered Weird Canada HQ with a glorious supply of sprinkles, will meld to your subpopconsciousness, becoming the immutable neon barnacle bursting infinite within every spurt of creative marvel. Nothing is more perennial than its majestic polyvinyl form blasting sinusoids at 45 rpm. "Wrinklecarver" is beyond hyperbole. TRIPPGRIP.
