Crystal Swells
Crystal Mountain Girls
(Self Released)
Vancouver, BC
::web/sounds::
From the crystallic necklace of Aaron Levin: Tilt the CDR at the right angle and a spasm of light will dance around your retina. An audible effect of the same calibre occurs after immediate Crustal Mountain Girls jammin'. Crystal Swell's aptly named CDR debut is a prismatic vortex of adjective-pop; a brilliant chromatic collage of splattered pop-punk. Their name had me thinking proto new age piano noddling, so don't make the same mistake. This ain't no Stephen Halpern joint.
