Futensil
Futensil
(Campaign For Infinity)
Montreal, QC
::web/sounds::
From the swampy high-life of Aaron Levin: Between the legs of a warped B-52s / Cramps hybranoid exists the maligned and twisted vibrations of Montreal's Futensil. Their atonal jungle of mystic wyrdabilly shivers beneath Emily's shrieks while Psychic Handshake CIO Graeme Langdon chants his way through a distant spell of psychedelic conjure. The overall emotional damage is an addicting aural experience for any futurotic voodoo voyeur.
