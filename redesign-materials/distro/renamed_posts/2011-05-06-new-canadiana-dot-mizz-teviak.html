From the heavy lids of Jessica Faulds:
Jesus, I wish I liked whiskey. A slow glass of the smoky stuff, a rocking chair, and DoT's Mizz Teviak wafting from the victrola: that's how this summer's sweltering twilights should look. Too bad I hate hard liquids, and this album isn't on vinyl â€” it's exactly the kind of dusky, slack-jointed folk that the occasional needle-pop could only accentuate. Without reaching outside of an acoustic palate, DoT does everything from syllable-splitting yarns to softly swirling paranoiac melodies to wurly kitsch. Compounded, these tracks will have you mesmerized on your porch, too heavy-lidded to slap the mosquitos eating your flesh. Bliss.
