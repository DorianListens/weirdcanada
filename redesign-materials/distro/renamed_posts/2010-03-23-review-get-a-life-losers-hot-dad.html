Get a Life Losers
Hot Dad
(Bruised Tongue)
Ottawa, ON
::web/sounds::
From the questionably-sexy dad-life of Aaron Levin: Take a wrong turn in Ottawa and you'll end up on a 15-minute death-march down dead-beat alley with a blizzard of psychedelic punk desolating your ears. Welcome to Hot Dad. Get a Life Losers' zebratic vortex of blown-out trash is a nihilistic tour through musical sadism; pulsing sounds of careening animals, the inner-howling of our trapped psychosis, and new levels of sonic weaponry. If you have 15 minutes to live and nothing but your fists and a bullshit attitude, Hot Dad is your death-row soundtrack. Certified SICCMADE.
